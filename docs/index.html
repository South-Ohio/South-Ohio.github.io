About

About

SOUA, is a community of individuals immigrating from Gujarat, India, who have came to the U.S. to seek opportunities in business and education for their families.


We organize various events & tournaments throughout the year such as Holi, Picnics, Ganpati, Navratri, Diwali, Cricket and Volleyball Tournaments in Southern Ohio.


Our goal is to promote positive mental health and improve social skills along with promoting physical health among adults and children.
